Things to do in Taguig City

Shopping

Explore popular shopping destinations like Bonifacio High Street, Venice Grand Canal Mall, and SM Aura Premier.

Dining

Experience a variety of cuisines at restaurants and food markets such as Mercato Centrale and McKinley Hill.

Parks and Recreation

Visit green spaces like Terra 28th Park, Track 30th Park, and the Mind Museum for outdoor activities and relaxation.

Cultural Sites

Discover cultural landmarks such as museums and traditional dances indigenous to the Philippines.

Nightlife

Enjoy vibrant nightlife scenes at clubs, bars, and lounges in areas like Bonifacio Global City and Uptown Bonifacio.

Sports

Participate in sports and recreational activities at facilities like the Manila Polo Club, McKinley Hill Stadium, golf courses.
